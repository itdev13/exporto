# Exporto - Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Landing Page for GoHighLevel Data Export Integration

---

## 1. Product Overview

### 1.1 Product Name
**Exporto** - The Missing Export Button for GoHighLevel

### 1.2 One-Liner
"Export what GoHighLevel won't let you - contacts with notes, conversations, tasks, and more."

### 1.3 Target Audience
- GoHighLevel sub-account users (agencies, marketers, business owners)
- Users frustrated with GHL's limited export capabilities
- Businesses needing to backup, migrate, or analyze their GHL data
- Agencies managing multiple client accounts who need data portability

### 1.4 Core Problem Statement
GoHighLevel lacks critical export functionality:
- **Cannot export contacts WITH their notes** - Users lose valuable context
- **Cannot export conversations** - Chat history is locked in the platform
- **Limited data portability** - Makes it hard to backup, migrate, or analyze data

### 1.5 Solution
Exporto is a standalone app with its own dashboard that connects to GoHighLevel via the GHL Marketplace. Users install the integration in their sub-account, then access Exporto's dashboard to select exactly what they want to export - from contacts with notes, to conversations, to tasks. All the powerful export capabilities that GHL doesn't provide natively, in one clean interface.

### 1.6 Key Features (Priority Order)
1. **Export Contacts with Notes** ⭐ (Primary selling point)
2. **Export Conversations** ⭐ (Primary selling point)
3. Export Tasks
4. Export Notes (standalone)
5. Export to CSV/Excel formats
6. Scheduled/automated exports (future)

## 7. Copywriting Guidelines

### 7.1 Tone
- **Empathetic**: We understand the frustration
- **Direct**: Clear about what we do and don't do
- **Friendly**: Casual but professional
- **Confident**: We solve a real problem

### 7.2 Key Messages to Reinforce
1. "GHL can't do this, we can" - Highlight the gap
2. "Your data, unlocked" - Emphasize data ownership
3. "Simple and fast" - No complexity
4. "Free to start" - Low barrier to entry

### 7.3 Words to Use
- Export, unlock, download, backup
- Contacts, notes, conversations
- Simple, fast, easy, minutes
- Free, no credit card

### 7.4 Words to Avoid
- Complex, technical jargon
- "Sync" (we export, not sync)
- Negative comparisons to GHL (stay positive)
